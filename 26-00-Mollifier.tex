A \emph{mollifier} is a smooth function $f\colon \R^n \to \R$ such 
\begin{enumerate}
\item $f(x)\ge 0$ for all $x\in \R^n$,
\item $f$ is normalized such that 
$$
  \int_{\R^n} f(x) dx = 1,
$$
\item $f(0)=1$.
\end{enumerate}
