integrating t⁢a⁢nx over [0,π2]


Note that what is meant by ∫0π2tan⁡x⁢d⁢x is actually limt→π2-⁡∫0ttan⁡x⁢d⁢x, since tan⁡x is defined on [0,π2) but not at π2.

∫0π2tan⁡x⁢d⁢x=limt→π2-⁡∫0ttan⁡x⁢d⁢x=limt→π2-⁡ln⁡|sec⁡x||0t=limt→π2-⁡ln⁡|sec⁡t|-ln⁡|sec⁡0|=limt→π2-⁡ln⁡|sec⁡t|=∞.
