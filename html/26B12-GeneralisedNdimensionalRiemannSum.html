Generalised N-dimensional Riemann Sum


Let I=[a1,b1]×⋯×[aN,bN] be an N-cell in ℝN. For each j=1,…,N, let aj=tj,0<…<tj,N=bj be a partition Pj of [aj,bj]. We define a partition P of I as

P:=P1×⋯×PN

Each partition P of I generates a subdivision of I (denoted by (Iν)ν) of the form

Iν=[t1,j,t1,j+1]×⋯×[tN,k,tN,k+1]

Let f:U→ℝM be such that I⊂U, and let (Iν)ν be the corresponding subdivision of a partition P of I. For each ν, choose xν∈Iν. Define

S⁢(f,P):=∑νf⁢(xν)⁢μ⁢(I⁢ν)

As the Riemann sum of f corresponding to the partition P.

A partition Q of I is called a refinement of P if P⊂Q.
