Theorem 1.
dd⁢x⁢(sin⁡x)=cos⁡x
Proof.
dd⁢x⁢(sin⁡x) =limh→0⁡sin⁡(x+h)-sin⁡xh
=limh→0⁡sin⁡x⁢cos⁡h+cos⁡x⁢sin⁡h-sin⁡xh by addition formula for sine
=limh→0⁡sin⁡x⁢(cos⁡h-1)+cos⁡x⁢sin⁡hh
=limh→0⁡(sin⁡x⋅cos⁡h-1h+cos⁡x⋅sin⁡hh)
=sin⁡x⁢(limh→0⁡cos⁡h-1h)+cos⁡x⁢(limh→0⁡sin⁡hh) by this entry (http://planetmath.org/LimitRulesOfFunctions)
=sin⁡x⋅0+cos⁡x⋅1 by this theorem (http://planetmath.org/LimitOfDisplaystyleFracsinXxAsXApproaches0) and its corollary (http://planetmath.org/LimitOfDisplaystyleFrac1CosXxAsXApproaches0)
=cos⁡x

∎

Theorem 2.
dd⁢x⁢(cos⁡x)=-sin⁡x
Proof.
dd⁢x⁢(cos⁡x) =limh→0⁡cos⁡(x+h)-cos⁡xh
=limh→0⁡cos⁡x⁢cos⁡h-sin⁡x⁢sin⁡h-cos⁡xh by addition formula for cosine (http://planetmath.org/AdditionFormulaForCosine)
=limh→0⁡cos⁡x⁢(cos⁡h-1)+sin⁡x⁢sin⁡hh
=limh→0⁡(cos⁡x⋅cos⁡h-1h-sin⁡x⋅sin⁡hh)
=cos⁡x⁢(limh→0⁡cos⁡h-1h)-sin⁡x⁢(limh→0⁡sin⁡hh) by this entry (http://planetmath.org/LimitRulesOfFunctions)
=cos⁡x⋅0-sin⁡x⋅1 by this theorem (http://planetmath.org/LimitOfDisplaystyleFracsinXxAsXApproaches0) and its corollary (http://planetmath.org/LimitOfDisplaystyleFrac1CosXxAsXApproaches0)
=-sin⁡x

∎
