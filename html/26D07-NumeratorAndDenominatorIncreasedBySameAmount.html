numerator and denominator increased by same amount


Let the positive fraction ab be altered by adding a positive number δ to both a and b.  Then

ab<a+δb+δ if a<b,
ab>a+δb+δ if a>b.

The asserted inequalities follow from the identity

ab-a+δb+δ=(a-b)⁢δb2+b⁢δ.
