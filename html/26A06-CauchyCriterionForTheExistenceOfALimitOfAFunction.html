Cauchy criterion for the existence of a limit of a function


Theorem 1.

Let S be a set and B a filter basis in S. A function f:S→R possesses limit on B, iff for every ϵ>0 there exists X∈B such that the oscillation of f on X is less than ϵ.

For a proof of this theorem see[1].
