alternative proof of derivative of xn


The typical derivative formula

d⁢fd⁢x=limh→0⁡f⁢(x+h)-f⁢(x)h

combined with the binomial theorem yield an alternative way to prove that

dd⁢x⁢(xn)=n⁢xn-1

for any positive integer n.

Proof.

dd⁢x⁢(xn)=limh→0⁡(x+h)n-xnh=limh→0⁡(∑j=0n(nj)⁢xj⁢hn-j)-xnh=limh→0⁡xn+n⁢xn-1⁢h+h2⁢(∑j=0n-2(nj)⁢xj⁢hn-2-j)-xnh=limh→0⁡n⁢xn-1⁢h+h2⁢∑j=0n-2(nj)⁢xj⁢hn-2-jh=limh→0⁡(n⁢xn-1+h⁢∑j=0n-2(nj)⁢xj⁢hn-2-j)=n⁢xn-1

∎
