Fejer kernel


The Fejer kernel Fn of order n is defined as

Fn⁢(t)=1n⁢∑k=0n-1Dk⁢(t),

where Dn is the Dirichlet kernel of order n. The Fejer kernel can be written as

Fn⁢(t)=1n⁢(sin⁡n⁢t2sin⁡t2)2. (1)

Proof: Since

Dn⁢(t)=sin⁡((n+12)⁢t)sin⁡t2

we have

sin⁡t2⁢Dn⁢(t)=sin⁡((n+12)⁢t).

Therefore

n⁢sin2⁡t2⁢Fn⁢(t) =∑k=0n-1sin⁡((k+12)⁢t)⁢sin⁡t2
=12∑k=0n-1(coskt-cos((k+1)t)
=12⁢(1-cos⁡n⁢t)
=sin2⁡n⁢t2.

From this follows equation (1).
