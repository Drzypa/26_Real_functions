Gronwall’s lemma


If, for t0≤t≤t1, ϕ⁢(t)≥0 and ψ⁢(t)≥0 are continuous functions such that the inequality

ϕ⁢(t)≤K+L⁢∫t0tψ⁢(s)⁢ϕ⁢(s)⁢𝑑s

holds on t0≤t≤t1, with K and L positive constants, then

ϕ⁢(t)≤K⁢exp⁡(L⁢∫t0tψ⁢(s)⁢𝑑s)

on t0≤t≤t1.
