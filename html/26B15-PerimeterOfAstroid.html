perimeter of astroid


The astroid

x23+y23=a23

can be presented in the parametric form

x=a⁢cos3⁡φ,y=a⁢sin3⁡φ,

where the polar angle φ gets the values from 0 to 2⁢π. The curve consists of four congruent arcs, one of which is obtained letting  0≦φ≦π2. Thus the whole perimeter of the astroid is

s=4⁢∫0π2(d⁢xd⁢φ)2+(d⁢yd⁢φ)2⁢𝑑φ.

This expression is easily simplified to

s=12⁢a⁢∫0π2sin⁡φ⁢cos⁡φ⁢d⁢φ

giving the result

s=6⁢a⁢∫0π2sin⁡2⁢φ⁢d⁢φ=-3⁢a⁢/0π2⁡cos⁡2⁢φ=6⁢a.
