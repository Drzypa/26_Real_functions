arc length


Arclength is the of a section of a differentiable curve. Finding the length of an arc is useful in many applications, for the length of a curve can represent distance traveled, work, etc. It is commonly represented as S or the differential d⁢s if one is differentiating or integrating with respect to change in arclength.
