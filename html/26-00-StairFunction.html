stair function


A stair function is a real function of the form

ϕ=∑k=1nak⁢χIk (1)

where  ak∈ℝ,  χIk is the characteristic function, k=1,2,…,n and I1,I2,…⁢In are bounded, pairwise disjoint intervals, for some n∈ℕ.
