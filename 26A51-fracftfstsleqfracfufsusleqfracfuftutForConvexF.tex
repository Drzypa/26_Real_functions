If $f$ is convex in $(a, b)$ and if $a<s<t<u<b$, then
\begin{equation}
    \displaystyle
    \frac{f(t)-f(s)}{t-s}\leq\frac{f(u)-f(s)}{u-s}\leq\frac{f(u)-f(t)}{u-t}.
\end{equation}
