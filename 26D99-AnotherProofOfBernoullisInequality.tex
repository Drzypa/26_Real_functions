For fixed $x>0,\ x\neq 1$ the function
$$
w_x(r)=\int_1^x t^{r-1} dt=
\begin{cases} \frac{x^r-1}{r}\quad& r\neq 0\\
\log x\quad& r=0
\end{cases}
$$
is strictly increasing.\\
Bernoulli inequality is equivalent to
$$ w_{1+x}(r) > (<)\ w_{1+x}(1)$$
