Let $I = [a_1, b_1]\times\cdots\times[a_n,b_n]$ be an $n$-cell in $\R^n$. Then the {\em Jordan content} (denoted $\mu(I)$) of $I$ is defined as
\[
  \mu(I) := \prod_{j=1}^n(b_j-a_j).
\]
