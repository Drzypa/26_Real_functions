Let $(a_n)$ be a sequence of real numbers. Then the series
$$\sum_{n=1}^\infty a_n$$
converges absolutely if
$$\limsup_{n\to\infty} \left|\frac{a_{n+1}}{a_n}\right|<1$$ 
and diverges if
$$\liminf_{n\to\infty} \left|\frac{a_{n+1}}{a_n}\right|>1.$$
